About Faviont

The revolution of fashion has come! Introducing Faviont, an NFT-based Marketplace for you to explore, sell, buy, and curate digital fashion & Metaverse fashion items. Formally known as “Faviont Markets”, this project is dedicated to creating a marketplace where fashionistas, digital fashion designers, and people from all walks of life can exchange digital fashion files as NFTs.

We strongly believe that digital fashion is the future we should embrace. We invite you to participate in our journey to revolutionize fashion!

More About Faviont

1000 Unique NFT Faviont Rings (2 phase)

There will be a total of 2 phases to Faviont Rings with total supply of 1000. (500 per phase).

Faviont Rings NFTs will be created and minted by people both within and outside the community. These NFTs are what you would call “Fashion NFTs”.

What makes these Faviont Rings special compared to other NFTs is the Dividend Distribution feature. What that entail is, those who hold these NFTs will gain consistent quarterly dividends from sales that occur in the Faviont Marketplace.
